# The Daily 5 — Guided Audio Script

**Duration:** 5 minutes
**Lesson:** 6.6 — The Habit, Not The Goal
**Voice:** Clean, efficient, no wasted words. This is a daily practice — the athlete will hear it dozens or hundreds of times. It needs to hold up to repetition. No motivational speeches. Just the drill.
**Music:** None.

---

## Script

[2-second silence]

Five minutes. Five tools. One each. This is The Daily 5.

[2-second pause]

**Minute One: Breathing.**

Three cycles of 6-2-7. Start now.

In... two... three... four... five... six. Hold... two. Out... two... three... four... five... six... seven.

[2-second pause]

In... two... three... four... five... six. Hold... two. Out... two... three... four... five... six... seven.

[2-second pause]

In... two... three... four... five... six. Hold... two. Out... two... three... four... five... six... seven.

[3-second pause]

**Minute Two: Highlight Reel.**

One scene. Your most powerful training or racing memory. First person. Full sensory detail. Go there now.

[10-second pause]

Feel the power. Feel the strength. Feel the version of you that showed up that day.

[10-second pause]

See yourself executing. Not the outcome — the execution. The form, the effort, the commitment.

[15-second pause]

Let it fade.

[3-second pause]

**Minute Three: Performance Statements.**

Say your three statements. Technical. Pain response. Process. Say them now — out loud if you can.

[8-second pause]

Again. With conviction.

[8-second pause]

One more time. These are your answers to the chimp. Mean them.

[8-second pause]

**Minute Four: Schema Statement.**

Who are you as an athlete? Say it. Your strength and your aspiration. One sentence.

[8-second pause]

Say it again. Slower. Let it land.

[10-second pause]

That's the target. That's who shows up tomorrow.

[5-second pause]

**Minute Five: Gratitude.**

One thing from today. Something in your training, your body, your sport, your life that you're grateful for. Not a big thing. Not a life-changing thing. Just one real thing.

Name it now.

[15-second pause]

Hold it for a moment. Then let it go.

[10-second pause]

That's The Daily 5. Breathing. Reel. Statements. Schema. Gratitude. Five minutes. Every day.

The tools work. But only if you use them.

[2-second silence]

[End]
