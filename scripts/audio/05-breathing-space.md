# 3-Minute Breathing Space — Guided Audio Script

**Duration:** 3 minutes
**Lesson:** 3.5 — Detachment in Action
**Voice:** Quiet. Minimal. This is the shortest exercise — every word earns its place. Think single sentences with long pauses between.
**Music:** None.

---

## Script

[2-second silence]

Three minutes. Three phases. Awareness, gathering, expansion.

[2-second pause]

**Minute one: Awareness.**

What's here right now? Don't change anything. Just notice.

What thoughts are running? Name them without following them. Just labels. "Planning." "Worrying." "Replaying."

[10-second pause]

What are you feeling emotionally? Not what you think you should feel. What's actually there. Anxiety. Frustration. Calm. Nothing. Whatever it is, name it.

[10-second pause]

What sensations are in your body? Tension, looseness, warmth, cold, pain, comfort. Scan from your feet to your head. Just notice. Don't fix.

[15-second pause]

**Minute two: Gathering.**

Now narrow your attention to one thing — your breathing. Feel the air enter your nose. Feel your chest rise. Feel the exhale leave.

Nothing else matters for the next sixty seconds. Just the breath. When your mind wanders — and it will — bring it back. No judgment. Just return.

[50-second pause]

**Minute three: Expansion.**

Now widen the lens. Keep the breath in the background, but expand your awareness outward. Your whole body. The space around you. The sounds. The temperature. The feeling of contact — your feet on the ground, your hands resting where they are.

Hold this expanded awareness. Present. Open. Not grasping at anything. Not pushing anything away. Just here.

[45-second pause]

That's it. Three minutes. You just practiced detachment — observing without reacting. The more you do this, the wider the gap gets between the chimp and the coach.

[2-second silence]

[End]
