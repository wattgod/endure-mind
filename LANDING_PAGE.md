### 4. THE DIFFERENTIATORS

**Headline:** This Is Not Another "Just Visualize Success" Course.

**Grid of 4:**

**First Principles, Not Topic Salad**
Every module starts with one idea in one sentence. Everything flows from it. No 47-concept information dump.

**Every Lesson Ends With Something You DO**
Named, repeatable protocols — not "reflect on your feelings." You'll practice these like you practice intervals.

**Built for the Psychology of Sustained Suffering**
Not team sports. Not ball sports. Endurance — where the deciding factor is your relationship with pain over hours.

**Backed by Research, Delivered by a Coach**
Synthesized from 4 sport psychologists, 15+ hours of academic content, and 6 years of field-tested coaching with real athletes.

---

### 5. THE THREE FORMATS

**Headline:** Learn It. Practice It. Absorb It.

**Course** — Deep learning
6 modules, ~30 lessons, video + worksheets + guided exercises. Go deep on the science and the practice.

**App** — Daily training
5 minutes/day. Streaks, spaced repetition, accountability circles. Your mental training plan, built into your routine.

**Audiobook** — Passive absorption
~6 hours. Listen while you ride, run, or drive. Stories, science, and tools narrated in a coaching voice you'll actually want to hear.

*They're complementary, not redundant. The audiobook sells the framework. The course provides the depth. The app creates the habit.*
