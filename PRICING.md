# Pricing Strategy

## Philosophy

The audiobook is the gateway. The bundle is the hero. The app is the retention engine.

Content products (audiobook, course) are one-time purchases — people expect to buy content once.
The app is a daily-use tool — people accept subscriptions for tools they use.
Don't mix these up.

## Product Tiers

### Audiobook — $19.99 (one-time)

**The gateway drug.**

- ~6 hours, narrative nonfiction + guided practice
- Distributed via Audible, Apple Books, Google Play, direct download
- Standalone — works without the course or app
- Every chapter plants a seed: "practice this with the app" / "go deeper in the course"
- Lowest commitment, highest reach

### Course — $79 (one-time)

**The deep dive.**

- Full 6-module, 30-lesson curriculum
- Video lessons (~10 min each)
- All 7 guided audio exercises
- Printable worksheets and checklists (Night-Before Checklist, Post-Race Debrief, etc.)
- Lifetime access
- Hosted on LMS (TBD — Teachable, Kajabi, or custom)

### Bundle — $119 (one-time)

**The hero product. Highest margin.**

- Audiobook + Full Course + 3 months of App (Athlete tier) included
- Saves ~$40 vs. buying everything separately
- The 3-month app trial builds the daily habit before asking for a subscription
- This is the product you push hardest

## Coaching Integration

The existing Endure coaching businesses are the distribution channel:

- **Summit Endurance Academy** — Bundle offered to all new coaching clients
- **Gravel God** — Audiobook as upsell to race prep content
- **Endure Labs app** — Cross-promotion within athlete-os-web

The Bundle effectively replaces the free Gravel Grit program with a paid product that's 10x deeper. Existing Gravel Grit recipients (6 years of athletes) are the warm list for launch.

## Launch Sequence

1. **Pre-launch**: Email warm list (Gravel Grit alumni) with "something bigger is coming"
2. **Audiobook first**: Lowest friction, widest reach, proves the framework
3. **Course + Bundle**: 2-4 weeks after audiobook — audiobook buyers get discount code
4. **App**: Launches with Bundle — free tier available to everyone, paid converts from course/audiobook users
5. **Pro tier**: 3-6 months post-launch — only after Coach Mode is built and tested

## Pricing Principles

- No monthly subscription for content. People hate it and it undersells the product.
- No "pay what you want." This is professional-grade content backed by research. Price it like it.
- No Patreon/membership model. Wrong audience — athletes buy gear and plans, not "support creators."
- Free tier must be genuinely useful (full Module 1) — not a crippled teaser.
- Bundle discount must be real and obvious — not a fake "was $199, now $119" game.
